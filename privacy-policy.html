Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how EasyShare handles your data.

üìã Overview

EasyShare is a browser extension that allows you to quickly upload and share files. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

üîç Information We Collect

Data Stored Locally

  • Extension Settings: Your preferences (upload service, notifications, auto-copy settings)
  • Upload History: List of your uploaded files and their download links (stored locally on your device)
  • Supabase Configuration: Your Supabase credentials (if you choose to configure them)

Data Processed During Upload

  • Files: Files you choose to upload are temporarily processed and stored on Supabase servers
  • File Metadata: File names, sizes, and upload timestamps
  • Download Links: Temporary URLs generated for file access

üõ°Ô∏è How We Use Your Information

  • File Sharing: To upload your files and generate shareable download links
  • User Experience: To remember your settings and show upload history
  • Notifications: To inform you about upload progress and completion
  • Clipboard: To automatically copy download links for your convenience

üîí Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

  • Settings and upload history are stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API
  • This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly share it
  • You can clear this data anytime through the extension settings

Cloud Storage

  • Uploaded files are stored on Supabase (a secure cloud storage service)
  • Files are automatically deleted after the expiration time (default: 1 hour)
  • Download links use signed URLs with time-limited access
  • We do not have permanent access to your uploaded files

üö´ What We Don't Do

  • No Analytics: We don't track your usage or collect analytics data
  • No Personal Data: We don't collect names, emails, or personal information
  • No File Content Analysis: We don't scan, analyze, or access your file contents
  • No Third-Party Sharing: We don't share your data with advertisers or third parties
  • No Permanent Storage: Files are automatically deleted after expiration

üåê Third-Party Services

EasyShare uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: For secure file storage and download link generation
  • Vercel: For API hosting and request processing

These services have their own privacy policies and security measures. We recommend reviewing their policies if you have concerns.

üë§ Your Rights and Control

  • Delete Data: Clear your upload history and settings anytime through the extension
  • File Control: Your uploaded files expire automatically (1-24 hours)
  • Settings Control: Modify or disable any extension features
  • Uninstall: Remove the extension to delete all local data

üîÑ Data Retention

  • Local Data: Stored until you clear it or uninstall the extension
  • Uploaded Files: Automatically deleted after 1-24 hours (configurable)
  • Download Links: Expire with the files (no permanent storage)

üîß Permissions Explanation

EasyShare requests the following permissions:

  • Storage: To save your settings and upload history locally
  • Notifications: To show upload progress and completion alerts
  • Clipboard Write: To automatically copy download links
  • Host Permissions: To communicate with Supabase and Vercel APIs

üì± Children's Privacy

EasyShare is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
